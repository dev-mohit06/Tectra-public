How it Works

Easy Steps to Get Started

Get up and running with Tectra in just a few simple steps. Our streamlined process ensures you can start managing your projects efficiently.

1

Sign Up

Create your account in minutes. Fill in your basic information and choose your role to get started.

2

Set Up Your Project

Create your first project by entering project details, timeline, and budget. Add team members and assign roles.

3

Manage Resources

Input your inventory, set up tracking for materials and equipment, and configure automated alerts for low stock.

4

Track Progress

Monitor tasks, track deadlines, and view real-time updates on your project's progress through our intuitive dashboard.

Features

Powerful Tools for Better Management

Streamline your workflow with our comprehensive suite of project management tools designed to keep your projects on track and under budget.

Budget Tracking

Keep track of project expenses in real-time with automated calculations, budget limits, and detailed expense reports.

Deadline Tracking

Stay on schedule with automated deadline reminders and a centralized calendar for tracking milestones.

Task Management

Organize tasks, assign team members, and track progress through an intuitive visual dashboard.

Inventory Management

Track materials and equipment usage with real-time updates and automated low-stock alerts.

Site Updates

Share real-time updates, photos, and videos from the field to maintain transparency across the team.

Quick Estimation

Generate accurate cost estimates instantly with detailed breakdowns for materials and labor.

Calendar

Visualize project schedules with an integrated calendar system for tasks, meetings, and milestones with easy drag-and-drop functionality.

Attendance Management

Track workforce attendance, monitor work hours, and generate detailed reports with automated check-in/out system.

Files Menu

Centralized storage for project documents with organized file management and controlled access permissions.
